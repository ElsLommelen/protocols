Welcome!

Protocols increase the quality of the fieldwork and thus the quality of scientific research, which must be transparent, traceable and applicable. They help make fieldwork more harmonized and more repeatable, reproducible and comparable. Standardization is also interesting for the Institute for Nature and Forest Research (INBO) from an efficiency point of view.

This site serves as a repository for the most recent versions of approved protocols used at INBO. On the left you will find the navigation to the different sections.
